<h3 style={{fontSize: '1.5rem', lineHeight: '2rem', paddingBottom: '8px', color: '#f0f0f0'}}>In this blog post I wanted to take it back a few years and showcase how I modeled my relational database for my Databases Course project</h3>

<p style={{fontSize: '1rem', lineHeight: '1.5rem', padding: '4px', color: '#f0f0f0'}}>When tasked with designing an application, we had a few requirements</p>
<ul style={{listStyle: 'disc', padding: '1rem', marginLeft: '1rem', marginTop: '0', marginBottom: '0'}}>
    <li>it has to obviously be connected to a database</li>
    <li>must have at least 4 tables</li>
    <li>minimum of 5 SQL queries</li>
</ul>

<p style={{fontSize: '1rem', lineHeight: '1.5rem', padding: '4px', color: '#f0f0f0'}}>I had the idea to make a social media application. Some features I wanted are:</p>
<ul style={{listStyle: 'disc', padding: '1rem', marginLeft: '1rem', marginTop: '0', marginBottom: '0'}}>
  <li>create an account</li>
  <li>make a post</li>
  <li>follow users</li>
  <li>make a comment</li>
  <li>like posts</li>
</ul>
